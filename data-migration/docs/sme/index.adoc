= SME book
:toc: left

The audience is the subject-matter expert reviewing data-mapping
decisions: crosswalks, pending/approved unification rules, the
program-detail tag dictionary, JSONB payload shapes, the drop list,
and how history is handled.

== Why your review matters

The migration code is mechanical. The decisions are not. Every wrong
crosswalk row, missed pending rule, or unmapped tag becomes a parity
warning at best and silently incorrect data at worst. Your sign-off is
the gate.
