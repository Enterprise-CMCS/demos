= Reference: comms templates

Pre-written stakeholder messages in `runbooks/comms/`. Send the right
one at the right time; do not improvise on cutover day.

[cols="2,2,4"]
|===
| When | Template | Audience

| Just before P1 freeze | `runbooks/comms/freeze_begin.md` | Users + stakeholders
| Freeze running long | `runbooks/comms/freeze_extended.md` | Stakeholders + on-call
| Just after P8 smoke green | `runbooks/comms/flip_complete.md` | Users + stakeholders (attach parity report)
| On rollback | `runbooks/comms/rollback.md` | Users + stakeholders + on-call
|===
